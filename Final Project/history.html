History of Artificial Intelligence

The Evolution of AI

The history of artificial intelligence (AI) can be traced back to ancient times, with myths and stories of artificial beings endowed with intelligence or consciousness by master craftsmen. As a field of study, AI officially began in the 1950s. Over the decades, AI has undergone significant transformations, influenced by advancements in computer science, mathematics, neuroscience, and more.

Key milestones in the history of AI include the development of the Turing Test, the creation of the first AI programs in the 1950s, the advent of machine learning in the 1980s, and the rise of deep learning in the 2010s. AI has transitioned from rule-based systems to neural networks that can learn and adapt, mimicking the human brain's complexity.

Today, AI is embedded in our daily lives, from virtual assistants to recommendation systems, transforming industries and society at large.
